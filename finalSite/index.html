Our idea

An integral component to public safety is reliable and effective emergency safety system. The most common type of public emergency safety system installed in every commercial and private building is, of course, a fire alarm system.

The Breezy

Our idea for our flagship product is called the Breezy. It is a smart fire alarm system powered by raspberry pi microcontrollers and some sensors. We made a small scale model to demonstrate the concept.
